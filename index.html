Nathan Organisation

Natan is a Hebrew word meaning ‘God has given’ and when I decided to start the journey of an NGO this is the name that came into my heart. I believe so much in giving back to the community, and I want to use this organization to give to the children of God those that are less

OUR VISION
Our vision is to help the female child and adolescence to see their potential by coaching and helping them grow in wisdom and in stature and in the favor of the Lord, therefore ensuring that girls and young women across the world grow to become powerful women in their societies.
OUR MISSION
We build the young woman and girl child to be a giver to the society and to know and understand that in the same way opportunities have been given to her, she can give to another girl.
GOALS
  • To ensure that young girls know who God is in their lives and the love He has for them
  • To provide her with a comprehensive education
  • To empower her to be confident and independent
  • To maintain good mental health for girls
  • To support her in reaching her full potential regardless of limitations
  • To connect her with opportunities in the business world and foster her growth
  • To nurture her development into a mature and responsible individual

Hi, I'm Kudzi...

This project is very dear to my heart it is quite personal if I should put it that way, ensuring that girl children have everything they need to survive and thrive in life will ensure that they are not taken for granted by a society that is ready to devour anyone who is seen as weak, especially from the opposite sex. If a girl is strong and independent, then they cannot be taken advantage of because they know who they are, their value, their strength and what they are capable of. This will not only be a onetime project, but it will grow to be a global powerhouse equipped to help girl children all over the world for generations to come, this organization will outlive me
